Meet Olia Batyr

Hey folks! Have you ever said to yourself, "Never say never!"? After having learnt four foreign languages I thought that I'm not capable of handling other languages. However, not that long ago I decided to become a web developer and started from front-end development. It turned out that the list of languages that I already know became even bigger by adding HTML and CSS to it (to be continued...) Now I discovered that the world of web development is so huge and exciting that sometimes it seems to be so hard to stop coding (have I just really said that?) Yes, it is still hard for me to believe that I got into this...It might be hard, mind-blowing and challenging but once I face any problems I do my best to deal with them and not run away from them. It is logical, inttersting and rewarding type of work that I enjoy and embrace every minute spent tackling difficulties I encounter.
